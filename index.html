What is Offmind?

Offmind

30 January 2022

"Offmind" was born with the idea of presenting and promoting paintings and works of art. Including images of works of art, information about the artists who created them, and details about the different styles featured.

Soon the possibility of buying or selling art paintings will be offered, we also have expert art historians who offer educational resources to learn more about art and its history. In short, if you are fascinated by art this is the place to go!
