The Author

David Portillo
January 1993

A few years ago, a young college student who shared a passion for art history realized that despite the vast amount of information that was available on this subject, much of it was scattered and difficult to access. So he decided to take action on the matter and create a website that would bring together all that information and make it accessible to anyone interested in art.

As an art historian, he knew very well the importance of telling the story behind each work of art, and decided that his website would be a place where anyone could find detailed and accurate information about each of the works of art that presented.

So he got down to business and began gathering information on different artists and works of art, and creating a database that included images and detailed descriptions of each one. He also created educational resources so people could learn more about art and its history, and began offering the ability to buy or sell art paintings through his website.

Little by little, his website began to succeed and attract a large number of people interested in art. The young college boy was very proud of what he had created, and was thrilled to see that his website was helping to spread the beauty and meaning of art to a wider audience.
